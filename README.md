# For Adwiteya — A Cinematic Love Story

An interactive romantic confession website built with pure HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript.

## How to Open

**Option 1 — Double-click**
Open `index.html` in Chrome, Edge, or Firefox.

**Option 2 — Local server (recommended for best performance)**
```bash
npx serve .
```
Then visit `http://localhost:3000`

## Experience Flow

1. **Opening** — Stars fade in, a glowing star transforms into a heart
2. **Story** — Word-by-word confession with a filling heart meter
3. **Reasons** — 28 interactive floating bubbles to tap
4. **Memories** — Glassmorphism cards with parallax / tilt
5. **Envelope** — Tap to open a handwritten love letter
6. **Pre-Proposal** — Heartbeat and zoom buildup
7. **Proposal** — "Will you be my girlfriend?" with YES / dodging NO
8. **Celebration** — Heart cracks open, particle explosion
9. **Garden** — Growing flowers, lanterns, butterflies, celebration messages (final scene)

## Music

Tap the 🎵 button (top-right) to choose a soundtrack. Music is **off by default**.

## Tips

- Best experienced fullscreen on desktop or mobile
- Tap/click through each scene at your own pace
- Wait 30 seconds on the garden scene for a hidden surprise ⭐
